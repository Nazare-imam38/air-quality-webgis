About Air Quality Monitor

This application provides real-time air quality data visualization using data from AQICN.org. The map shows air quality levels across the globe with color-coded indicators.

How to use:

  • Click anywhere on the map to see location details
  • Use the controls to switch between different data layers
  • Zoom and pan to explore different regions
  • Check the legend to understand air quality levels

Data Sources:

Air quality data is provided by AQICN.org and updated in real-time.
